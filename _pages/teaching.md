## Teaching Assistant — RWTH Aachen University (2022–2025)

During my PhD I served as a teaching assistant for several undergraduate and graduate physics courses. My responsibilities included leading tutorial sessions, preparing problem-solving classes, assisting students with assignments, and supporting course instructors during examinations.

### Courses

**Advanced Cosmology** — Master's level  
Winter Semester 2023, Winter Semester 2024  

Topics included inflationary cosmology, cosmic microwave background anisotropies, large-scale structure formation, and statistical properties of primordial perturbations.

**Statistical Physics and Thermodynamics** — Bachelor's level  
Summer Semester 2023  

Tutorial sessions focused on equilibrium statistical mechanics, thermodynamic potentials, ensembles, and applications to physical systems.

**Electrodynamics** — Bachelor's level  
Winter Semester 2025  

Covered Maxwell’s equations, electromagnetic waves, potentials and gauge transformations, and classical radiation.

**Master's Thesis Co-Supervision**

## Teaching and Outreach — SVNIT Surat (2017–2022)

During my undergraduate and master's studies at SVNIT Surat, I taught voluntary sessions aimed at students and the general public.

**Classical Mechanics (2020)**  

Delivered a short lecture series introducing Lagrangian and Hamiltonian mechanics, emphasizing conceptual understanding and problem-solving techniques for undergraduate students.

**Astrophysics Outreach and Public Telescope Session (2019)**  

Organized an introductory lecture on fundamental astrophysical concepts followed by a public telescope observation session designed for a non-technical audience.

These activities aimed to promote scientific curiosity and make astronomy accessible to the broader community.
